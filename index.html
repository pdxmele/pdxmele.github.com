Maps, bikes, and general nerdiness.

Hello world!

Welcome to my humble home page. I work as a GIS Technician at TriMet, where I assist with the new Map Trip Planner, an open source multi-modal trip planner built with OpenTripPlanner. You can generally find me editing OpenStreetMap, making web maps for fun and (non)profit(s), or riding one of my bikes around Portland, OR.
