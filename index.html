Maps, bikes, and general nerdiness.

Hello world!

Welcome to my humble home page. I'm Mele Sax-Barnett, and I manage the Customer Engineering team at Urban Airship. I also like to play with geographic data. I formerly worked as a GIS Technician at TriMet, where I assisted in the creation of the Map Trip Planner, an open source multi-modal trip planner built with OpenTripPlanner.

I'm also the current OpenStreetMap US Board Vice President. You can generally find me learning a new programming language, editing OpenStreetMap, making web maps for fun and (non)profit(s), doing yoga, running, or riding one of my bikes around Portland, OR.

If you use my side projects or presentations, please consider donating on Gittip to help me keep it up!
