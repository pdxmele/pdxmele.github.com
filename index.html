Hello world!

Welcome to my humble home page. I'm Mele Sax-Barnett, and I'm a Technical Support Engineer at Urban Airship. I'm also helping out a bit with location projects there. I formerly worked as a GIS Technician at TriMet, where I assisted in the creation of the new Map Trip Planner, an open source multi-modal trip planner built with OpenTripPlanner. You can generally find me editing OpenStreetMap, making web maps for fun and (non)profit(s), or riding one of my bikes around Portland, OR.
